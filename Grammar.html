NOUNS

A nouns represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.


KINDS OF NOUN


Nouns are classified as follows:

Proper: It is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea or thing.

Common: It is the name possessed by thing and general.

Collective: It is the name representing a group of things.

Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to void repretition of a noun

KIND OF PRONOUNS

Pronouns are classified as follows:

Personal pronoun: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitude of the noun

Interrogative pronoun: It stand as the answer to the question about a person, place or thing

Indefinite pronouns: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.

Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.

Demonstrative pronoun: A noun or pronoun is specified and indentified by it.

Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
